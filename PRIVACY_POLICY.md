# Privacy Policy for Fish Float

**Effective date:** July 19, 2026  
**Developer:** KTech Studio  
**Contact:** ktechstudio.support@gmail.com

KTech Studio ("we," "us," or "our") built Fish Float as a general-audience, family-friendly mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Fish Float.

## Information Fish Float Handles

Fish Float does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, phone number, address, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The game stores gameplay information on your device, such as:

- High scores and gameplay statistics
- Collected pearls and unlocked cosmetic items
- Equipped fish and game preferences
- Mission and achievement progress
- Sound settings and purchase entitlement status

This local gameplay information is used to operate the game and preserve your progress. It is not uploaded to a KTech Studio account or server. Removing the app may remove locally stored progress, subject to device backups and platform behavior.

## In-App Purchases

Fish Float offers optional in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. KTech Studio does not receive or store your full payment card or banking details.

Apple may process purchase information, transaction identifiers, storefront information, and account-related information under Apple's own privacy policies. Fish Float receives purchase confirmation and entitlement information needed to deliver purchased content, restore eligible purchases, and prevent duplicate delivery of consumable items.

## Optional Rewarded Advertising

Fish Float may offer optional rewarded advertisements. You can play the game without watching an advertisement. Rewarded advertisements are clearly labeled and are shown only after you choose to view one in exchange for an in-game benefit, such as a revive or bonus pearls.

When advertising is enabled, Fish Float may use Google AdMob and its consent tools. Depending on your region, device settings, consent choices, age-related settings, and the availability of advertising, Google and its partners may process information such as:

- Device and app identifiers
- IP address and general location derived from it
- Advertising interactions, impressions, and diagnostic information
- Device type, operating system, language, and app version
- Consent choices and fraud-prevention information

KTech Studio intends to configure advertising for a family-friendly experience, use age-appropriate content controls, avoid requiring advertisements, and request non-personalized or limited advertisements where appropriate. We do not sell personal information. Advertising availability may be restricted for some users or regions.

For information about Google's data practices, visit [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Consent and Privacy Choices

Where required, Fish Float will display a consent or privacy message before requesting advertisements. Available choices may depend on your location and applicable law. If a privacy-options control is required, you may use it from within the game to review or change available advertising choices.

You may also limit advertising identifiers or tracking through your device settings. Fish Float is intended to operate without permission to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies.

## Children and Families

Fish Float is a general-audience game designed to be suitable for families. It is not intended to collect personal contact information from children, and the game does not include account registration, public profiles, chat, or user-generated content.

Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of devices, in-app purchases, and optional rewarded advertising. If you believe a child has provided personal information directly to KTech Studio, contact us so we can review and, where appropriate, delete it.

## Data Sharing

We may share or allow information to be processed only as needed for:

- App Store purchase processing and restoration through Apple
- Optional advertising, consent management, measurement, and fraud prevention through Google AdMob and its authorized partners
- Legal compliance, protection of users, prevention of fraud, or enforcement of our rights

We do not rent or sell personal information to data brokers.

## Data Retention

Local gameplay information remains on your device until it is reset, removed by the operating system, or the app is deleted. Purchase records are retained by Apple according to Apple's policies and applicable law. Advertising and consent information is retained by Google and its partners according to their policies, configuration, and legal obligations.

## Your Rights and Requests

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of certain personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing.

Because KTech Studio does not operate player accounts or a gameplay-data server, most gameplay information can be removed by deleting the app or clearing its local data. For privacy questions or requests concerning information controlled by KTech Studio, email ktechstudio.support@gmail.com. We may need reasonable information to understand and verify a request.

Requests concerning Apple purchases or Google advertising data may also need to be directed to Apple or Google because those companies independently control certain information they process.

## Security

We use reasonable measures designed to protect information handled by the game. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## International Processing

Apple, Google, and their service providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies and applicable safeguards.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Fish Float's features, service providers, or legal obligations change. We will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy at the same public URL. Material changes may also be communicated within the game or through the App Store where appropriate.
